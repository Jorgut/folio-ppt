Folio · Wireframe Sheet

Print this sheet or use in browser · Each card = one slide wireframe · Fill in content before writing HTML

How to Use This Sheet

  1. Step 1 (IA): Complete your Deck Structure Document — know your narrative arc, content blocks, and page count.
  2. Step 2 (Lo-fi): On this sheet, sketch each slide's wireframe — just boxes and labels, no details.
  3. Step 3 (Mid-fi): Add grid annotations, font sizes, image ratios in the annotation area below each wireframe.
  4. Step 4 (Hi-fi): Fill in real content, exact spacing, and responsive breakpoints.
  5. Step 5: Tell Folio (via Claude / any AI agent) your content and style, then export to HTML / PPTX / PDF / Figma / IDML.
